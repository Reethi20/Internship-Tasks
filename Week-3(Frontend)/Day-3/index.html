Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a famous fantasy book series written by J. K. Rowling that tells the story of a young wizard named Harry who studies at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He makes close friends like Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger and learns about magic and courage. Throughout the series, Harry faces many challenges and battles the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. The story highlights themes of friendship, bravery and the power of love.
